Resolution S/RES/2712 — Humanitarian Pause

The Security Council, gravely concerned by the humanitarian situation, reaffirming its commitment to international humanitarian law...

1. Calls for immediate humanitarian pauses;
2. Demands safe passage for humanitarian aid;
3. Urges all parties to protect civilians;
4. Decides to remain actively seized of the matter.
