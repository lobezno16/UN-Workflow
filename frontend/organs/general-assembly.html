Adopted Resolutions

A/RES/78/1

Climate Action Acceleration

The General Assembly, recalling the Paris Agreement and its goals, deeply concerned by the accelerating impacts of climate change, recognizing the need for urgent and ambitious action...

1. Calls upon all Member States to enhance their nationally determined contributions;
2. Urges developed countries to fulfill climate finance commitments;
3. Encourages technology transfer for climate adaptation;
4. Requests the Secretary-General to report on implementation progress.
